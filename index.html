multimedia

multimedia is the media that uses multiple forms of information content and information processing e.g. text, audio,graphics, animations,video,interactivity to inform or entertain the user.

Advantages and disadvantages of multimedia in webpages

Advantages
  1. Greater Immersion
  2. Enhanced page rankings for SEO
  3. Better Branding

disadvantages
  1. Potential Damage to Search engine Ranking
  2. Loading Times
  3. compatibility
  4. May increase cost and time
